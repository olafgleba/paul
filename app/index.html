paul.dev

paul.dev is just my personal frontend development environment. It relies on libraries and extensions such as Bower, Grunt, Sass and the inuit.css framework as the foundation to build up the real css stuff. Instead to fiddle about repetitive, boring tasks, it allows me to focus on what i do like most: Building websites and apps.

Introduction

paul.dev just reflects my way of doing things, my approach to facilitate starting new projects. Maybe someone find this frontend helpful. As a point to start but also as a inspiration to build and tweak it up for your own purposes.
